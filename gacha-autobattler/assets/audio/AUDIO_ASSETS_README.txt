================================================================================
                        AUDIO ASSETS GUIDE
================================================================================

Place downloaded audio files in the appropriate folders below.
Supported formats: .wav (SFX), .ogg (Music)

================================================================================
SFX FOLDER: assets/audio/sfx/
================================================================================

Required files:

  ui_click.wav
    - Short button/menu click sound
    - Source: Kenney UI Audio pack
    - Duration: ~0.1s

  attack_hit.wav
    - Combat impact/hit sound
    - Source: Kenney Impact Sounds or RPG Audio
    - Duration: ~0.2-0.5s

  heal.wav
    - Healing/restoration magic sound
    - Source: Kenney RPG Audio (magic/spell sounds)
    - Duration: ~0.3-0.5s

  unit_place.wav
    - Unit deployed to battlefield
    - Source: Kenney UI Audio or Impact Sounds
    - Duration: ~0.2-0.3s

  unit_death.wav
    - Unit defeated/knockout sound
    - Source: Kenney Impact Sounds
    - Duration: ~0.3-0.5s

  victory_fanfare.wav
    - Battle won celebration
    - Source: Pixabay or Freesound
    - Duration: ~1-2s

  defeat.wav
    - Battle lost sound
    - Source: Pixabay or OpenGameArt
    - Duration: ~1-2s

  summon_buildup.wav
    - Gacha anticipation/charging sound
    - Source: Pixabay "summon" or Kenney Digital Audio
    - Duration: ~0.5-1s

  summon_reveal_3star.wav
    - Common unit reveal (subtle)
    - Source: Kenney RPG Audio
    - Duration: ~0.3-0.5s

  summon_reveal_4star.wav
    - Rare unit reveal (medium impact)
    - Source: Kenney RPG Audio
    - Duration: ~0.3-0.5s

  summon_reveal_5star.wav
    - Legendary unit reveal (dramatic)
    - Source: Kenney RPG Audio or Pixabay
    - Duration: ~0.5-1s

================================================================================
MUSIC FOLDER: assets/audio/music/
================================================================================

Required files (use .ogg format for smaller file size):

  menu_theme.ogg
    - Main menu background music
    - Source: OpenGameArt CC0 Fantasy Music
    - Should loop seamlessly

  battle_theme.ogg
    - Combat/battle music
    - Source: OpenGameArt Battle Themes
    - Should loop seamlessly

  victory_theme.ogg
    - Victory celebration music
    - Source: OpenGameArt CC0 Music
    - Can be short jingle or looping

  defeat_theme.ogg
    - Defeat/game over music
    - Source: OpenGameArt CC0 Music
    - Can be short or looping

NOTES
================================================================================

- All Kenney assets are CC0 (public domain) - no attribution required
- OpenGameArt CC0 assets are also public domain
- Pixabay sounds are royalty-free, no attribution required
- Convert MP3 to WAV/OGG using Audacity (free) if needed
- Keep SFX short and punchy for better game feel
- Music should loop seamlessly (check loop points)
